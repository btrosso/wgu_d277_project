About Montgomery:

Located along the banks of the Alabama River, Montgomery, the capital city of Alabama, is a vibrant blend of historyand culture. It had a significant role in the Civil Rights Movement. Montgomery bore witness to pivotal moments in American history, including Rosa Parks' inspiring stand against segregation.
